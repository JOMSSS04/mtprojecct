SIWAWER FESTIVAL OF VINTAR

In the great rivers and towering mountains of Vintar, the Imalawa tribe thrives under Chieftain Bulan. When he names his eldest son, Dayaw, as the next leader, jealousy burns within the younger son, Bileg, who longs for the title himself. While hunting, an argument erupts between the brothers, leading Bileg to strike down Dayaw. Consumed by guilt, he flees into the wilderness, only to be swallowed by a violent earthquake that plunges him into the terrifying underworld, where monstrous creatures await. Just as Bileg faces his reckoning, Dayaw—who had survived—rescues him. Together, they battle the underworlds evil spirits and return home. Humbled by his brothers love, Bileg finally understands that true leadership is not about power, but honor and unity.
